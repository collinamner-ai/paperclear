Quick Start Guide

📸

1. Capture

Point your camera at a document and tap the capture button. Frame the entire page.

✨

2. Enhance

Choose from Clean, B&W, or Sharp filters to improve readability. You can try different options.

📄

3. Preview & Review

Review all pages before saving. Swipe through thumbnails and delete any bad captures.

💾

4. Save & Organize

Save to folders (Tax, Medical, Receipts, etc.) and export as PDF to your device.

🔒

Privacy First

Everything stays on your device. No cloud, no accounts, no tracking. Ever.
